# Basket Analysis & Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents a *Power BI dashboard* focused on *sales performance* and *market basket analysis*.

It combines key business metrics with product association insights to answer:
- When does the business perform best?
- How valuable is each customer order?
- Which products are frequently purchased together?

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into *actionable insights that support better business decisions*.

---

## Dataset
- *Dataset used:* Superstore  
- Contains transactional data including orders, products, dates, and sales  

---

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance Metrics
- *Total Sales* → Overall revenue generated  
- *Best Performing Year* → Identifies peak yearly performance  
- *Top Sales Month* → Highlights strongest monthly performance  
- *Total Orders* → Total number of transactions  
- *Average Order Value (AOV)* → Revenue per order  

These KPIs provide a clear snapshot of overall business performance.

---

### Time Intelligence Analysis
- *Sales Trend by Month*
  - Visualized using a line chart  
  - Reveals seasonality and growth patterns over time  

---

### Market Basket Analysis
- *Support Calculation*
  - Measures how frequently each product appears in transactions  

- *Product Association Analysis*
  - Identifies products that are commonly purchased together  
  - Highlights opportunities for cross-selling and bundling  

This helps answer a key business question:
> “Which product combinations can drive higher revenue?”

---

## Power BI Techniques Used
- Data modeling  
- Measures and calculated columns

## Key Insights

- Certain periods consistently outperform others  
- A subset of products are frequently purchased together, revealing:

  - Product bundling strategies  
  - Potential for recommendation systems
